All of the subjects posted on this blog come directly from my experience in the games industry, as well as from conversations with coworkers, teachers, students, and other industry professionals. I never use AI tools to generate ideas for articles.

I do sometimes use AI tools for research purposes, but I take great care to vet the information they provide. As a general rule, I always prompt for citations and tend to get annoyed whenever that instruction is ignored.

All of the text on this blog is drafted by me. However, I do use AI tools for editing purposes such as spell-checking, grammar, and punctuation. Occasionally, I may also use them to assess whether sentences or paragraphs should be rearranged for better flow, or to compare different ways of expressing an idea to make it easier to read.

This page is inspired by [Damola Morenikeji’s AI manifesto](https://www.bydamo.la/p/ai-manifesto).
